Background

After receiving my diploma from the Harris Institute in Toronto, Ontario, I worked as a producer and tracking engineer for artists from all over Toronto. More recently I worked at the famous Blue Room studios in Atlanta, Georgia where I mentored under top 40-mix/tracking engineer James Kang. I am available to track your next project. I bring industry standards and work flow to your project. I am proficient in tracking into Pro Tools and I have extensive knowledge working with analog consoles and patch bays. In addition to working with musicians and recording artists, I have worked with voice over actors in the studio. I bring a professional attitude and I collaborate to get the best performance possible from the talent.
