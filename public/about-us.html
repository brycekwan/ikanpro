About Ikan Productions

My name is Adrian Kwan and I am a sound professional who provides services that covers a broad range of sound needs. My obsession with sound and sound quality began with my love for music. Music made me aspire to move people with my own selection of music. I began as a DJ for my school dances and house parties. I started out using an old boom box with dual cassette decks. I soon purchased my first belt drive turntable and saved up enough to eventually obtain a pair of Technic 1200 direct drive turntables.

After performing as a DJ, I turned my attention to production and audio engineering. My desire to further my career in sound lead me to enrolled into the Producing Engineering program at the Harris Institute for the Performing Arts in Toronto, Ontario. I finished an intense one-year diploma program and started my company Ikan Productions. I began working on productions for artists in the great city of Toronto and also for artists in the United States.

In 2010, I decided to continue my growth and attended the Savannah College of Arts and Design in Savannah, Georgia. I completed my Bachelorâ€™s of Fine Arts in Sound Design for film and television. Since graduating, I have worked as a freelance sound designer, a dialogue editor and as a location recordist for a variety of animated shorts and independent films. I also worked as a tracking engineer at the Blue Room Studios in Atlanta, Georgia.

I now reside in my home city of Toronto, Canada and I continue to provide services to clients of the GTA as well as clients across the globe ranging from sound editing, sound design, tracking, production and being your event DJ. I offer competitive pricing and I am always prepared to listen to your needs. I understand that working in the creative field, we must be open to the ideas and the creative direction of all those involved so that we may produce the best and most complete project.
